About TOP2000 App

My name is Rick Neeft and I'm the developer of the (Unoffical) TOP2000 App for Windows, Android and as of now since now also for MS-DOS. Why a MS-DOS application? Well because I can and since we were all in lockdown due to the Corona virus, it seems a fun to go back to the roots, the MS-DOS era.

The application is developed in Visual Basic 1.0 for DOS whitin DOSBox. In the download sections you will find an overview to different pages on GitHub.
