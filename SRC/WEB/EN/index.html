Top 2000 App for MS-DOS

With the Top 2000 app you have a complete overview of tracks of all the editions, since 1999, within one application.

This is the most amazing and user-friendly app for the Top 2000 especially created for MS-DOS. The app was designed for a more fluid experience, as well as to provide users an easier way to keep track of the list.

The Top 2000 is an annual broadcasting program of the Dutch radio station Radio 2. The show begins broadcasting at 9 AM on Christmas Days and continues until midnight of New Year's Eve (CET Time).
